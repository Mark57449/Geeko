Showing off her sweet smile and beautiful Vintage Dress, Max Factory's new 1/7 scale Hatsune Miku figure is sure to get fans pumped for her next concert!

The highlight of this figure is obviously the shiny and stylish costume, which is the Vintage Dress module design from Hatsune Miku -Project DIVA- F 2nd! The pearly paintwork adds a romantic feel to the atmosphere, and with the crystal microphone in her hand, it’s easy to imagine her up on stage performing.


Her heartwarming smile is also sure to steal fans' hearts!


Bring the concert to your home by preordering at Tokyo Otaku Mode, with an estimated release of Jul. 2019.
